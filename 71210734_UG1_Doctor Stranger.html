Synopsis

The series will revolve around Park Hoon, a South Korean who was captured
with his father and grows up in North Korea. He is trained to become a genius doctor,
and though Park Hoon lands a job at the best South Korean hospital after defecting, he is treated as a foreigner.
To find his lost North Korean first love, Song Jae Hee, he does anything to make money.
